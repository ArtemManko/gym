    Service

    What we do

    We train the best athletes. We will help you develop such qualities as:.

    Stamina

    The fight can be very long. If both athletes have the same strength, the match may be delayed. In this case, we also develop your stamina to be 100% ready.

    Power

    Strength is the main quality in arm wrestling. Through proper training, we will help you develop your strength to the maximum. Having strong hands is good not only in competitions, but you can also open any jar:)

    Speed

    The fight begins at the command of the referee. It is very important to have a good speed to finish the match faster. We also need speed so that the initial movement of the hand is stronger.
